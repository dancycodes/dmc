## What It Does
Provides WhatsApp contact integration and social media links on the cook's tenant landing page. Includes a floating WhatsApp chat button that opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message to the cook, social media links (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok) in the footer, and a click-to-call phone number. These are always accessible to visitors, providing a direct communication channel with the cook.

---

## Who Uses It
| Role | Interaction |
|------|-------------|
| Client (guest or authenticated) | Contacts the cook via WhatsApp, views social profiles, or calls directly |
| Cook | Receives WhatsApp messages, calls, and social engagement from clients |

---

## User Scenarios
### Scenario 1: WhatsApp floating button
A visitor sees a green WhatsApp icon floating in the bottom-right corner of the page. They click it. WhatsApp opens (app or web) with a pre-filled message: "Hi Chef Latifa, I'm interested in ordering from DancyMeals!" addressed to the cook's phone number.

### Scenario 2: Social media links in footer
The visitor scrolls to the footer and sees icons for Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. They click the Instagram icon and are taken to the cook's Instagram profile in a new tab.

### Scenario 3: Click-to-call
The footer also shows the cook's phone number: "+237 6XX XXX XXX". On mobile, clicking it initiates a phone call. On desktop, clicking it copies the number to clipboard.

### Scenario 4: Cook with no social links
A cook has only a WhatsApp number configured but no social media links. The footer shows only the WhatsApp number and phone. Social media icon section is hidden.

### Scenario 5: Mobile WhatsApp button
On mobile, the floating WhatsApp button is slightly smaller and positioned to not overlap with other navigation elements. Tapping it opens the WhatsApp app directly.

## Acceptance Criteria
- **Given** a cook with a WhatsApp number, **when** the page loads, **then** a floating WhatsApp button is visible
- **Given** the visitor clicks the WhatsApp button, **when** WhatsApp opens, **then** the cook's number and pre-filled message are correct
- **Given** a cook with Instagram and Facebook configured, **when** viewing the footer, **then** both social icons are displayed and link to correct profiles
- **Given** a cook with no social links, **when** viewing the footer, **then** social media section is hidden
- **Given** a mobile visitor, **when** tapping the phone number, **then** a phone call is initiated
- **Given** a desktop visitor, **when** clicking the phone number, **then** the number is copied to clipboard with a success toast

---

## Verification Steps
1. Verify the floating WhatsApp button appears on the tenant landing page
2. Click the WhatsApp button and verify it opens WhatsApp with the correct number and message
3. Verify social media links in the footer open correct URLs in new tabs
4. Verify only configured social links are shown
5. Test click-to-call on mobile
6. Test copy-to-clipboard on desktop with success toast
7. Verify the WhatsApp button does not obstruct other elements on scroll

---

## Edge Cases & Exceptions
| Condition | Expected Behavior |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Cook has no phone number | WhatsApp button hidden; phone section hidden |
| Cook's phone number is invalid | WhatsApp link opens but may not connect; no client-side validation |
| Social media URL is invalid | Link opens but leads to an error page on the social platform |
| WhatsApp not installed on mobile | Opens WhatsApp Web in browser instead |
| Page is very long | WhatsApp button stays fixed in viewport as user scrolls |

---

## UI/UX Notes
- WhatsApp floating button: green circle with WhatsApp icon, fixed position bottom-right
- Button has a subtle pulse animation on first load to draw attention
- Social links in footer as icon-only buttons with hover tooltips showing platform name
- Phone number displayed with a phone icon, styled as a link
- Footer section: "Contact Us" or "Get in Touch" (localized) header
- Spacing ensures the floating button does not overlap footer content when scrolled to bottom
